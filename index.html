Our School Facilities

Our school provides excellent facilities for students’ overall development. The computer lab helps students learn modern technology and improve their skills. The transport facility ensures safe and convenient travel for students. The green playground provides a healthy environment for games, exercise, and outdoor activities.

Our School Bus

A school bus carries students safely from home to school and back. It follows a fixed route and ensures students reach school on time. It is a safe and convenient means of transport for children.

Computer Lab

Our school computer lab helps students learn computer skills and technology. It has many computers with internet and educational software. Students use the lab to gain knowledge and improve their practical learning.

Green Playground

Our school playground is a place where students play and enjoy different games. It helps students stay healthy and active. The playground also develops teamwork and sportsmanship among students.
